Wrist Drop Test

Test Setup

Before dropping the wrist, set up the drop surface on top of your work bench. Make sure that the area is clear. Warn others in the room that you are about to drop the wrist. This test will be loud.

Running Test

After each drop, click "Continue"

You may drop the wrist multiple times in each configuration. After you click "Continue", the computer will prompt you with the next drop. Follow the pictures shown on this screen when dropping the wrist.

Safety

Keep your hands and other body parts away from the wrist while it is dropping.

Inspecting for Damage

If at any time you see scratches, burrs or dents in the wrist, stop the test immediately by clicking cancel. The computer will automatically fail the test if the tilt wrist looses power or drops packets.
