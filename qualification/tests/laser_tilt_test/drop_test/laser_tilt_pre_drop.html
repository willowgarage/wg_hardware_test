Laser Tilt Drop Test

Test Setup

Before dropping the laser tilt, set up the drop surface on top of your work bench. Make sure that the area is clear. Warn others in the room that you are about to drop the sled. This test will be loud.

Make sure that the Hokuyo is removed. Do not drop the Hokuyo.

Running Test

After each drop, click "Continue"

You may drop the sled multiple times in each configuration. After you click "Continue", the computer will prompt you with the next drop. Follow the pictures shown on this screen when dropping the sled.

Safety

Keep your hands and other body parts away from the sled while it is dropping.

Inspecting for Damage

If at any time you see scratches, burrs or dents in the sled, stop the test immediately by clicking cancel. The computer will automatically fail the test if the tilt sled looses power or drops packets.
