Prosilica Test

Test Setup

Plug in Prosilica. Mount it to the stand as shown.

Running Test

Focus the camera according to instructions below. These instructions are posted for your convienence.

(1)Loosen the iris screw.
(2)Set the iris to be wide open. You can look into the lens and see how open the iris is.
(3)Tighten the iris screw.
(4)Loosen the focus screw.
(5)Adjust the focus until the graph is as high as possible.
(6)Tighten the focus screw without changing the focus. If the focus changes while you are tightening, return to step 4.
(7)Loosen the iris screw.
(8)Set the iris to the 8 marking.
(9)Tighten the iris screw.

Press 'Continue' to proceed.
