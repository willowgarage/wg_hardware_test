Head Drop Test

Test Setup

Before dropping the head, set up the drop surface on top of your work bench. Make sure that the area is clear. Warn others in the room that you are about to drop the head. This test will be loud.

Remove Instruments and Secure all Cables

Make sure that the all instruments removed. Do not drop with cameras or sensors installed.

Running Test

After each drop, click "Continue"

You may drop the head multiple times in each configuration. After you click "Continue", the computer will prompt you with the next drop. Follow the pictures shown on this screen when dropping the head.

Safety

Keep your hands and other body parts away from the head while it is dropping.

Inspecting for Damage

If at any time you see scratches, burrs or dents in the head, stop the test immediately by clicking cancel. The computer will automatically fail the test if the tilt head looses power or drops packets.
